About Us

Kerala Skill Express(KSE) is a remarkable event organised in collaboration with Gtech mulearn and Kerala Knowledge Economy Mission and hosted by IEEE SB LBSITW . As part of this extraordinary gathering, we proudly unveil 'Unclutched' - Let go of your code at fingertips, a hackathon that will captivate the minds of participants. Step into the realm of Unclutched, a stirring 24-hour hackathon meticulously crafted to provide skill enhancement and upskillment opportunities for all participants. With a beginner-friendly approach, it is the perfect platform for aspiring innovators to embark on a transformative journey of growth and learning by combining the realms of creativity and technology where innovation thrives and collaboration sparks groundbreaking ideas. This exceptional opportunity will provide participants with the exact tools they need to craft a splendid future, empowering them to lead their finest lives, engage with a vibrant community of innovators, engineers, and professionals, fostering valuable connections and collaborations and it serves as a unique platform for engineers to interact, introspect, innovate, ideate, and unleash their creativity.
